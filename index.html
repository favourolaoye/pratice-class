Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk (optional, for fluffiness)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp butter or oi

Instructions

  1. Crack 2-3 eggs into a bowl.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Whisk the eggs until the yolks and whites are completely combined.
  4. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan.
  5. Whisk in 2 tbsp of milk, if desired, for fluffiness.
